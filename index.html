Banana Bread

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 5 people

Ingredients Quantity

  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup of wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3 bananas
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup of melted butter

Process

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 C
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients (first four items) in a large bowl
  3. In a separate bowl, mash the bananas until soft
  4. In a another seperate bowl, beat the egg until it's liquid
  5. Mix in the egg, vanilla extract, and melted butter with the bananas
  6. Pour the dry ingredients in with the mashed wet ones
  7. Mix thoroughly so that there is dry, powdery residue left
  8. Butter up a loaf pan so that the bread doesn't stick
  9. Pour mix into the pan, and stick in the heated oven
  10. Wait for 50-60 minutes and take out
  11. Place on rack so that moisture doesn't accumulate
  12. Enjoy
